## Slide 4: Solution Comparison (Layout: `split`)
### Title: Closed Cloud vs. Open Gamma Compiler
### Left Column (Vulnerable Cloud):
- **Data exposure** — drafts stored on third-party remote databases.
- **Stalling asset queues** — single slow visual queries block the entire presentation.
- **Provider lock-in** — restricted to specific model subscriptions.
### Right Column (Open Gamma):
- **100% privacy control** — drafts securely saved locally in lightweight SQLite.
- **Timeout protection** — 10-second Promise.race timeout guards prevent queue hangs.
- **BYOK model freedom** — standard REST and CLI support for Gemini, Claude, or local engines.
### Notes:
- data-slide-type: split
- data-slug: architecture-comparison
<aside class="notes">
  Walk through the contrast. Emphasize that the 10-second queue timeout guard and local SQLite storage make Open Gamma robust under any system conditions.
